National News

Article-1 : Assam Hit by 5.8 Magnitude Earthquake

A magnitude 5.8 earthquake struck Assam (Udalguri) with a shallow depth of 5 km. Tremors were felt strongly in Guwahati. Thankfully, no damage or casualties have been reported so far.

Article-2 : IMD Warns of Heavy Rain & Severe Flooding

The India Meteorological Department has issued alerts for intense rainfall in the Northeast and Maharashtra. Manipur is already reeling under severe flooding. Citizens are being advised to take precautions in affected regions.

Article-3 : PM Modi Starts National Deepwater Exploration Mission

India has launched a National Deepwater Exploration Mission to assess potential oil and gas reserves in its maritime zones, aiming to boost energy self-reliance and harness deep sea resources.

Article-4 : Push to Develop Green Hydrogen Innovation Hub

The government is focusing on making India a hub for green hydrogen technology, aiming to generate high-value jobs. Initiatives include support programmes for research, manufacturing, and clean energy infrastructure.

Article-5 : Nationwide Anti-Drug Campaign Launched in Schools

CBSE schools across India have started a campaign against drug abuse, involving workshops, awareness sessions and parent-teacher discussions. The aim is to foster a drug-free and healthy learning environment.
